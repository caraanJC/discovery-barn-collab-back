How to Play

Objective:

Open all the cards within the time limit.

At the start of the game, player will be given 5 seconds to see and remember all the cards in the collection.

For selected cards to be opened, player will have to match a pair of cards. 1 point will be given for every matched pair.

Player will have 30 seconds to open all the cards in the game. Goodluck!
